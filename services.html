Our Services

ہماری خدمات

Daily Dastarkhwan

We serve fresh, nutritious meals every day to hundreds of deserving individuals. Our Dastarkhwan (community kitchen) operates from Surjani Town, providing breakfast, lunch, and dinner to the homeless, laborers, and low-income families.

Meals served daily: 150+

Beneficiaries (yearly): 18,000+

Marriage Assistance

We help underprivileged couples solemnize their nikah by providing financial support for dowry, venue, and feast. So far, we have assisted over 800 families in organizing dignified weddings.

Families helped (monthly): 800+

Education Support

We provide school fees, books, and uniforms to children from low-income families. We also run adult literacy classes to empower the community through education.

Medical Aid

We arrange free medical camps, provide medicines, and bear hospital expenses for critical patients. Our network of volunteer doctors ensures basic healthcare reaches those who cannot afford it.
